Calma is One Of The Best Food Service.

Calma was opened on 27 October 2018. Chef / Owner Callista Nweke began making meals and selling them to people out of a small store at the Federal University Birnin Kebbi, Kebbi State. Today, Calma’s beloved restaurant shop celebrates three years of classic, made from scratch Nigerian cooking.
